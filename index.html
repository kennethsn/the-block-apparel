Our Approach

To Ensure our Clients can have custom personalization with rapid turnaround, we streamline our orders into three key steps: Tell us about your project. We'll design a digital sample. We will ship it directly to you.

Gather Your Information

To begin your next order with us, we first want to understand your project's designs, occasion, and garment selections in order to provide you with a quote.
Check out our pricing guide and garment catalog to get familiar with the various options we offer. When you have made a selection, reach out to our team! Email us at tbpercussion1@gmail.com

Digitize Your Designs

Once we have gathered necessary design assets and decoration instructions, we will prepare a digital sample of your finalized designs. Upon your approval, we will use these renderings to personalize your garments.

Ship Your Order

We offer rapid turnaround time and are flexible with orders of any size. Once your designs are finalized, our team will discuss shipping options that best fit your needs.
